Who We Are?

Mad About Bricks is a club that came to life when 2 friends, who shared the same passion about lego decided to create a club for lego fans. The main purpose of the club is to have fun, make new friends and create amazing memories while building legos. Our club is 100% free and open to all ages, we meet up every Saturday morning and all you need to bring is your passion for lego. We have new sets ready to build every week but you can also bring your own.

Join Us!

And? Does the thought of building legos and making new friends appeal to you? If yes don't hesitate and contact us! You can simply give us a call or fill our contact us form and we will get in touch.
